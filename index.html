• Lossless Youths

    Lossless Youths explores a generation growing up surrounded by advanced technology and endless digital memories. Every moment is recorded, stored, and replayed, leaving little room for forgetting or moving on. The story questions whether preserving everything also preserves pain and regret. As the characters mature, they struggle to define identity without loss. It is a reflection on memory, growth, and the cost of never letting go.

  • Estranged Bond

    Estranged Bond tells the story of two siblings separated by conflict and time. Although distance has hardened their relationship, shared history continues to pull them together. The narrative moves between past memories and present consequences. It highlights how unresolved emotions quietly shape adult lives. At its core, the story is about reconciliation and the courage to confront the past.

  • The Gate Keeper

    The Gate Keeper follows a lone guardian stationed at the edge of a forgotten world. His role is simple yet heavy with responsibility: decide who may pass and who must turn back. Over time, isolation blurs his sense of duty and morality. Each decision carries irreversible consequences. The story examines power, responsibility, and the quiet weight of choice.

  • Last Trace of Us

    Last Trace of Us is set in a world where humanity has nearly vanished. Small signs of life remain in abandoned cities and silent machines. The protagonists search for meaning in what was left behind. Their journey reveals how civilization echoes long after people are gone. It is a quiet meditation on legacy, survival, and remembrance.

  • Urban Decay

    Urban Decay captures the slow collapse of once-thriving cities. Technology still hums in the background, even as human presence fades. Nature begins reclaiming concrete and steel. The story contrasts progress with neglect and abandonment. It invites reflection on how societies rise, peak, and quietly disappear.

  • The Migration

    The Migration follows communities forced to move as their environment changes. Harsh climates and failing systems leave them with no choice but to travel forward. The journey is dangerous and uncertain. Along the way, traditions are tested and identities reshaped. The story focuses on resilience, adaptation, and hope in motion.
